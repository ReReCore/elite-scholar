📖 How Sync Works
1. Enter any memorable phrase as your Sync Key when you first open the app.
2. A private cloud storage space is created for that key — no account needed.
3. Every change you make pushes to the cloud within 3 seconds automatically.
4. On a new device: open the app → enter the exact same key → done.
5. If you lose your key, your local browser data is still safe — you just can't sync.
